During the first three months of the full-scale invasion, the Ministry of Digital Transformation came up with, launched, and saw positive results from numerous unplanned digital projects. Each project, in its way, helped bring Ukraine closer to victory. The Minister of Digital Transformation, Mykhailo Fedorov, himself presented these projects at Davos, Switzerland's 2022 World Economic Forum.

My task was to collaborate with the video editor to create detailed and clear video presentations of three projects for the international delegations.

To do this, I gathered information on each project, highlighted the key points, wrote the copy for the voiceovers and captions, and provided the video editor with storyboards and shot breakdowns.
