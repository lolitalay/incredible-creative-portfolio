Core concept, naming, user research, personas, problem statements, user journey maps

I’m always learning and growing, and currently, as part of the Google UX Design course, I am developing an app from scratch for low-cost one-night stays in New York City within a network of friends or friends of friends. At this stage, I’ve created the core concept of the service, and its name, and conducted user research. The project is ongoing, so soon you can expect design iterations: wireframes, prototypes, and mockups.
