Slogan

Rabota.ua

Rabota.ua, Ukraine's largest job search platform with over 4M monthly users, shifted its positioning from presenting dry job and candidate statistics to creating an emotionally driven Website for finding opportunities.

My task was to help the company motivate people through a slogan, encouraging them to look for career opportunities that would lead to a happy life, rather than just for a survival job.

For the rebranding launch, I proposed the campaign slogan "Why not you?". The essence of this message is that every day, thousands of Ukrainians improve their lives through opportunities they find on Rabota.ua. So, why it's someone else, but not you?
