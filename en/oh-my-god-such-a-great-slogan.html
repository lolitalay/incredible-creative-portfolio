Slogan

Rabota.ua

Ukraine's top job search platform

Rabota.ua, Ukraine's largest job search platform with over 4M monthly users, shifted its positioning to become a Website for finding opportunities. My task was to create a motivational slogan for this transition.

For the rebranding launch, I proposed the campaign slogan "Why not you?". Thousands of Ukrainians are improving their lives with opportunities from Rabota.ua. Why not you?
