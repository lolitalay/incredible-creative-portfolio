Saphira Agency was a young marketing creative agency that worked for American, Ukrainian, and Israeli markets with positioning: the client at the center of processes.
At the beginning of their journey, the agency reached out to me to create guidelines and a brand tone of voice for external client communication across all touchpoints.
001
Initially, in line with the brand brief and strategy, I identified 4 key tones of voice traits and detailed each one.
002
After that, I outlined the languages the brand communicates in, considering its multinational audience.
003
Afterward, I outlined clear points on how the brand communicates and how it doesn't, ensuring that copywriters create further communication and maintain a consistent writing style. Here, I ensured the language was clear and understandable to the reader while adhering to writing standards and reflecting the brand's persona.
