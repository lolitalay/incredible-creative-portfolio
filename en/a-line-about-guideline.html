Saphira Agency

↓↓↓

Saphira Agency was a young marketing creative agency with 50 employees in main office in Kyiv, Ukraine. Worked for American, Latin American, Ukrainian, and Israeli markets.
At the beginning of their journey, the agency reached out to me for creating guidlines and a brad tone-of-voice.
The agency's founder had a clear vision for positioning of the agency: putting the client at the center of all processes. He had several other businesses and had worked with creative agencies from the client's side. As a client himself, he didn't like that agencies typically focus on internal decision-making, don't always listen to the client's preferences, communicate poorly about project status, and may completely chage the team during long-term collaboration.
That's why the agency's approach was clear to him:
client is at the center of all processes.
001
Initially, in line with the brand brief and strategy, I outlined the brand persona, providing a brief description of the character and their behavior.
002
Based on this, I identified 5 key tone of voice traits and detailed each one.
003
Afterward, I outlined clear points on how the brand communicates and how it doesn't, ensuring that copywriters creating further communication maintain a consistent writing style.
004
