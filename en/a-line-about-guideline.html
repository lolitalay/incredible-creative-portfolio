Saphira Agency was a young marketing creative agency with 50 employees in the main office in Kyiv, Ukraine. Worked for American, Latin American, Ukrainian, and Israeli markets.
At the beginning of their journey, the agency reached out to me for creating guidelines and a brand tone of voice.
The agency's founder had a clear vision for positioning the agency: putting the client at the center of all processes. He had several other businesses and had worked with creative agencies from the client's side. As a client himself, he didn't like that agencies typically focus on internal decision-making, don't always listen to the client's preferences, communicate poorly about project status, and may completely change the team during long-term collaboration.
That's why the agency's approach was clear to him:
client is at the center of all processes.
