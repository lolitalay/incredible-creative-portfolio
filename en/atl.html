Outdoor Advertising: concept, copy, actor selection, organizing and overseeing the shoot

State Payments in Diia

Let's take a look↓

Diia and Visa entered into a technical collaboration, officially enabling the option to pay state payments with Visa cards through the Diia application.

This technical collaboration evolved into a communication one, as it was necessary to inform the public that they could now pay state payments through the government application without any additional fees or third-party banking applications.
My tasks were:
✅

Create the main concept for the campaign's billboards


✅

Develop the copy in the brand's official voice, conveying the key message to the audience: 'State payments can be made online with a card directly in Diia'


✅

Approve the visual elements in collaboration with the creative designer: selecting visuals and models for the shoot


✅

Organize the actual photo shoot
