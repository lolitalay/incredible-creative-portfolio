Slogan

Rabota.ua

Rabota.ua
is one of Ukraine's largest job search websites, with a monthly count of over 4 million unique visitors and a total of 10+ million visitors.


During my internship at a top creative agency in Kyiv, Rabota.ua approached them for a rebranding project.

At that time, there was a problem where Ukrainian job search websites' advertisements lacked emotions, focusing solely on numbers: job vacancies here, candidates there. Brands were measured by the number of offers rather than the number of happy people. Consequently, people were searching not for their calling but for a paycheck. Not cool, right?
Then, the agency's strategists devised the positioning "The website for searching opportunities" for Rabota.ua. I came up with the slogan for the advertising campaign, which shifted people's motivation from searching for a paycheck to searching for the best job for them with one simple question: "Why not you?".
